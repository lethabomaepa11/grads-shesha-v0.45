## Adding a header

- Go to configuration studio
- Right click on your module
- <img width="366" height="226" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/841d1713-2dc8-4546-a15b-6dbe841e5a57" />
---
- Then click on "expose existing"
- You will see the form below, click on the header from the Shesha Module
- <img width="1135" height="503" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2f2f43d2-2cc7-488b-af0a-aa07f62e1403" />
---
- You can then click ok, then search for the header in your forms
- Edit it as you like.

### How to make it viewable in the frontend
- Open the adminportal folder in your IDE
- Go to `src/app/(main)/layout.tsx`
- Then edit the MainLayout to be like below
- <img width="842" height="241" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7d7fef7e-3997-4ab1-b1c5-65e5e2e1a8de" />
- The `module` should be your module name where you created the header form in
- You can view changes in the frontend
